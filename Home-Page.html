NEUST is a highly-regarded educational institution located in the province of Nueva Ecija in the Philippines. It was established in 1906 as the Gabaldon School, and since then, has grown into a university that offers a wide range of academic programs in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), as well as in other disciplines such as business, education, and agriculture.

NEUST is known for its commitment to providing quality education to its students, as well as for its research initiatives and community outreach programs. The university has a strong faculty composed of experts in their respective fields, and it offers modern facilities and resources to support student learning.
